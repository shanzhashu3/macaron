Let's find out more about macaron

01.
gradually add 35g sugar, egg whites are whisked until stiff-peaked meringue forms.

02.
Sifted almonds and icing sugar are also mixed with raw egg whites to form a paste. The meringue and almond paste are mixed together to form the macaron mixture.

03.
Added food coloring, then mix it .You’ll know it’s ready when it has a “lava-like” consistency, forming a thick ribbon that slowly blends back into itself when drizzled from your spatula.

04.
Transfer batter to a piping bag fitted with a round tip.Pipe onto a sheet of parchment paper fixed to a baking sheet. Tap the tray several times to remove air bubbles. Allow to sit for 40-60 minutes so the macarons form a skin.You should be able to touch the shell and feel a dry surface.

05.
Heat oven to 300F. Bake for 12-15 minutes. Allow to cool for about 10 minutes on the pan then transfer to a rack to cool completely. Pipe Jam onto the back of half the shells. Form a sandwich and enjoy!
